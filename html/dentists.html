Meet the Dentists

At Example Dental, our team of experienced and compassionate dentists is dedicated to providing exceptional care for you and your family. Get to know the professionals behind your smile:

Emily Carter, DDS

With over 15 years of experience, Dr. Carter is known for her gentle touch and patient-focused approach. She graduated from The University of Toronto with honors and specializes in preventive and restorative dentistry. Dr. Carter is passionate about creating a comfortable environment where patients feel empowered to take charge of their oral health. When she's not in the office, she enjoys hiking with her family and volunteering at local dental outreach programs.

Dr. Michael Torres, DMD

Dr. Torres brings a wealth of expertise in cosmetic dentistry, helping patients achieve their dream smiles with cutting-edge techniques. A graduate of McGill University, he has a keen eye for detail and a reputation for delivering stunning results. Dr. Torres stays up-to-date with the latest advancements through continuing education. Outside of work, he enjoys photography, traveling, and cheering for his favorite sports teams.

Dr. Sophia Nguyen, DDS

Pediatric dentistry is Dr. Nguyen's specialty, and her warm, friendly demeanor makes her a favorite among our youngest patients. She earned her degree from The University of British Columbia and has additional training in working with children and special needs patients. Dr. Nguyen's goal is to make every dental visit a fun and positive experience. In her free time, she loves crafting, baking, and spending time with her two dogs.

Dr. Anand Patel, DMD

A highly skilled dentist with a focus on advanced procedures such as dental implants and oral surgery, Dr. Patel ensures that patients receive comprehensive care under one roof. He graduated from The Schulich School of Medicine and Dentisty at Western University and is committed to helping patients restore function and confidence in their smiles. Dr. Patel enjoys mentoring young dentists, playing guitar, and exploring new cuisines in his downtime.

Our team is here to provide the care, expertise, and compassion you deserve. We look forward to helping you achieve a lifetime of healthy, beautiful smiles!
